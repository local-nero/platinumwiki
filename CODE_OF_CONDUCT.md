# Code of Conduct 🏆

## Our Standard

We want to maintain a fair, competitive, and toxic-free community for all demonlist players, submitters, and staff.

## Rules for Players and Submitters 🕹️

* **No Cheating**: Any form of hacking, macroing, or modification that alters physics or gives an unfair advantage is strictly banned.
* **Legitimate Records**: Submissions must include valid video proof with audible clicks, accurate frame counters, and required raw footage when requested.
* **Respect Staff**: List editors and moderators judge records based on objective rules. Disagreements must be handled through the official appeal system, not by harassing staff.
* **No Toxicity**: Harassment, hate speech, and targeted drama toward other players or list members will result in a leaderboard ban.

## Rules for List Staff ⚖️

* **Impartiality**: Review all records objectively regardless of personal opinions about the player.
* **Transparency**: Clearly state the reasons for rejecting a record or moving a level on the list.
* **Privacy**: Keep internal staff discussions, unreleased level movements, and private player details confidential.

## Enforcement 🔨

Violations of this code may lead to:
* Rejection of submitted records.
* Temporary or permanent ban from the leaderboard.
* Removal from the community Discord server or website staff.

---

*To report a violation or appeal a decision, contact the list administrators through our official Discord server.*
